## Design System

- **Layout:** Split-screen (50/50).
- **Left Panel (Hero/Branding):**
    - **Background:** Deep Obsidian (#020617).
    - **Visual:** Abstract teal neural network abstraction.
    - **Components:** Branding block, "Kinetic Intelligence" pill, and "Active Optimization" glass widget.
- **Right Panel (Authentication):**
    - **Background:** White (#FFFFFF) in Light Mode / Slate 950 (#020617) in Dark Mode.
    - **Form:** Login headline, credentials inputs (12px radius), and Vivid Orange (#EA580C) CTA.
    - **Constraint:** No Social Login buttons.

## Corner Radius & Elevation
- **Cards/Widgets:** 24px.
- **Buttons/Inputs:** 12px.
- **Tonal Layering:** Use luminosity shifts; avoid 1px borders for left panel elements.
